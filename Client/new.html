SQUAT

Squats are dynamic, explosive movements involving lowering the body into a deep bend at the knees and hips, followed by a rapid return to the standing position, effectively incorporating cardiovascular and strength elements for an intense and efficient exercise.

JUMPING SQUATS

Jumping squats add an explosive element to traditional squats by incorporating a jump at the top of the movement. This engages additional muscles and enhances the cardiovascular benefits of the exercise.
